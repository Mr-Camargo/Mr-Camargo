This is Mirayoki.


Ongoing 2020 - Now 12+ Hours

Mirayoki is an open source, multi-purpose Discord chatbot designed from the ground up for speed, efficency and privacy.

It is based on the Discord.js library and runs its own separate operative system, MiraOS.

Mirayoki is on an very early development stage, and while more information is not available right now, new data and highlights will be added to this page.
